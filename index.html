Email: yue.zheng@ntu.edu.sg

I am currently a Research Fellow in Centre for Integrated Circuits and Systems (CICS) in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU, Singapore). I received the Ph.D degree from NTU, Singapore in 2020 and the B.Eng degree from Shanghai University (SHU), China in 2015. My research interests include hardware security, Physical Unclonable Function (PUF), security protocols, random number generator, physical layer security, and etc. Please refer to research and publication pages for more details.
